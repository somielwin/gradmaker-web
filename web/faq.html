Frequently Asked Questions

  • Get Familiar with the Terms

    Variable Life Product

    This is a product with investment and insurance combined. GradMaker is an example of a variable life product, so you have an investment and insurance at the same time. This is the advantage of GradMaker versus investments that you usually get from a bank. With GradMaker, you get insurance protection that you wouldn’t otherwise get from a pure investment product.

    Premium

    This is the amount that you pay for an investment/insurance policy. In short, what you pay us (Manulife) for your GradMaker Policy.

    Account Value

    This is the worth of your investment at a given point in time.

    Death Benefit

    If you die, this is the amount of money that your beneficiary gets.

    Beneficiary

    This is the person who receives the death benefit.

    Trustee

    This is the person who holds or administers the death benefit for a minor beneficiary.

    Surrender Charges

    This is the fee deducted from your Account Value if you withdraw this within a certain period.

    Maturity Date

    This is the date upon which the Account Value will be paid out to you.

  • Understand GradMaker

    What is GradMaker?

    The GradMaker product is a Single Premium product, meaning the policy is paid in full at the time of purchase. You are able to purchase additional policies at any time.

    What are the product benefits of GradMaker?

    With GradMaker, you can invest in a diversified portfolio of stocks and bonds which can help your money achieve long-term growth.
    Your family is also protected so that in case of your untimely demise, your child or your chosen beneficiary will get at least 125% of your single premium even if the market is down.

    What is the difference of GradMaker with Pre-need Education Plans?

    Most of the companies who offered old Pre-Need Education plans promised and guaranteed to fully cover the cost of tuition fee, whatever that amount may be, as long as clients paid their premiums. However, come actual payout time, those companies were confronted with high tuition fees that exceeded their projections, and they no longer deliver their promise to their plan holders. GradMaker is an entirely different concept, because GradMaker is a product that allows you to invest in a professionally-managed fund. This way, you can take advantage of the growth in the economy and beat inflation – but as with any investment, we fully disclose to our prospect clients that there are no guarantees. We are transparent on how your money is invested and on how the fund performs every day. Through GradMaker, you can also monitor how much your money is worth at any given time, and you can even withdraw your money when you need it.
